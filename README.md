# 📱 Student Life Hub (Temporary Name)

An all-in-one, holistic daily organizer designed specifically for students to balance academics, physical health, recreation, and spiritual well-being. 

Unlike standard planners that only focus on tasks, this application bridges the gap between productivity and personal life, helping students manage their study sessions, work hours, recreation, medicine schedules, and daily prayers in one cohesive space.

---

## ✨ Features

*   **🎓 Academic & Work Tracker:** Log study hours, manage assignments, and track work shifts.
*   **🕌 Prayer & Spiritual Companion:** Integrated prayer times and reminders to help maintain spiritual discipline amidst a busy schedule.
*   **💊 Health & Wellness:** Reliable medicine logs and water tracking to ensure physical health never takes a backseat.
*   **🎯 Smart Reminders:** Custom notifications for upcoming deadlines, tasks, and daily routines.
*   **🎮 Play & Recreation:** Dedicated slots for downtime and hobbies to prevent burnout and promote a healthy work-life balance.
